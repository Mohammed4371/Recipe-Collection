Fish Fry Recipe

Here's a simple step-by-step recipe for making a delicious fish fry:

Ingredients:

  • 4 fish fillets (such as tilapia, cod, or any firm white fish)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup buttermilk (or regular milk)
  • 1 large egg
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)
  • Fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Prepare the Fish:

  • Rinse the fish fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.

2. Set Up Breading Stations:

  • In one bowl, mix together the flour, cornmeal, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper.
  • In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk and egg.

3. Bread the Fish:

  • Dip each fish fillet into the buttermilk mixture, ensuring it's coated well.
  • Dredge the fillet in the flour mixture, pressing lightly to adhere. Ensure each fillet is fully coated with the breading.

4. Heat the Oil:

  • In a large skillet or frying pan, heat about 1/2 inch of vegetable oil over medium heat. The oil is ready when it shimmers and a small piece of bread dropped in it sizzles.

5. Fry the Fish:

  • Carefully place the breaded fish fillets in the hot oil. Fry in batches if necessary, without overcrowding the pan.
  • Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the fish is golden brown and crispy, and the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).

6. Drain and Serve:

  • Remove the fried fish from the skillet and drain on paper towels.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges and optionally garnish with fresh parsley.

Enjoy your crispy and flavorful fish fry!
